---
title: "Out of the comfort zone"
date: 2026-07-02
---

I'm not usually the kind of person who posts stuff publicly - nor in social media or other channels. I think I've just never really had the need to put out myself and my thought. 

This time it's sort of different. I've been wandering around thinking, ans it struck me that I've been very fortunate to live in a such age. 

Everyone in the society freely can express and together form this dynamic organism with accumulated knowledge base. 

And this time I want to share back to that organism and what I hope could be valuable to others, even though it really requires to go out of the comfort zone - as the cliché states.
